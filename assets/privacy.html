Privacy Policy for Jobs BD

Last updated: 12 July 2024

Jobs BD ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our mobile application (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, search for jobs, or communicate with us. This may include:

  • Personal information (e.g., name, email address, phone number)
  • Professional information (e.g., resume, work history, education)
  • Job preferences and search history

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our App
  • Match you with relevant job opportunities
  • Communicate with you about our App and services
  • Send you notifications about new job listings or application updates

3. Permissions We Request

Our App requires the following permissions:

  • Internet: To access job listings and communicate with our servers
  • Notifications: To send you updates about new job opportunities and application statuses

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information with:

  • Employers or recruiters when you apply for a job through our App
  • Service providers who perform services on our behalf
  • Law enforcement or other parties if required by law or to protect our rights

5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

6. Your Choices

You can update your account information or adjust your notification preferences within the App settings. You may also request to delete your account by contacting us.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
